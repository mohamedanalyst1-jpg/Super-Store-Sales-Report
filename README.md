📌 Project Overview

This report analyzes a fictional superstore's transactional data to uncover revenue patterns, customer behavior, product performance, and geographic distribution of sales. The report is structured across five dedicated pages, each focusing on a specific area of the business, with a central control page for seamless navigation.

🎯 Key Questions Answered


Who are the most valuable customers and what drives their purchasing behavior?
Which products and categories generate the most revenue and profit?
How do sales trends evolve over time — monthly, quarterly, annually?
Which store locations are the top performers?
Where are the biggest opportunities to improve margins or reduce losses?



📊 Report Pages

1. 🏠 Control Page

Central navigation hub with quick-access buttons to all report sections.

2. 👤 Customer Analysis


Customer segmentation by purchase frequency and revenue contribution
Key metrics: Total Customers, Average Order Value, Repeat Purchase Rate
Visuals: Clustered column charts showing customer spend distribution


3. 📦 Product Performance


Revenue and profit breakdown by product category and sub-category
Best-selling and lowest-performing products
Visuals: Bar charts, stacked area charts, horizontal bar comparisons


4. 📈 Sales Analysis


Sales trends over time (monthly/quarterly)
Regional sales distribution using treemaps
Visuals: Line charts, clustered column charts, treemaps


5. 🏪 Stores


Store-level performance comparison
Geographic sales distribution on an Azure Map
Visuals: Column chart (Sales by Store Name), clustered bar chart, map visual
